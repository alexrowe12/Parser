AN ABSOLUTE REFUSAL

“MELVILLE’S CHARACTER FITS IN WITH A LONG
TRADITION OF THE REFUSAL OF WORK. ANY WORKER
WITH ANY SENSE, OF COURSE, WANTS TO REFUSE THE
AUTHORITY OF THE BOSS, BUT BARTLEBY TAKES IT TO THE

EXTREME. HE DOES NOT OBJECT TO THIS OR THAT TASK,
NOR DOES HE OFFER ANY REASON FOR HIS REFUSAL—HE
JUST PASSIVELY AND ABSOLUTELY DECLINES.”

MICHAEL HARDT & ANTONIO NEGRI, EMPIRE
(HARVARD UNIVERSITY PRESS, 2000), 221

GENERAL STRIKE

“THE NEXT DAY | NOTICED THAT BARTLEBY DID NOTHING BUT STAND AT HIS
WINDOW IN HIS DEAD-WALL REVERY. UPON ASKING HIM WHY HE DID NOT
WRITE, HE SAID THAT HE HAD DECIDED UPON DOING NO MORE WRITING.

“WHY, HOW NOW? WHAT NEXT?” EXCLAIMED I, “DO NO MORE WRITING?”

“NO MORE.”
“AND WHAT IS THE REASON?”
“DO YOU NOT SEE THE REASON FOR YOURSELF,” HE INDIFFERENTLY REPLIED.

| LOOKED STEADFASTLY AT HIM, AND PERCEIVED THAT HIS EYES LOOKED DULL
AND GLAZED. INSTANTLY IT OCCURRED TO ME, THAT HIS UNEXAMPLED
DILIGENCE IN COPYING BY HIS DIM WINDOW FOR THE FIRST FEW WEEKS OF HIS
STAY WITH ME MIGHT HAVE TEMPORARILY IMPAIRED HIS VISION.” (MELVILLE,

“BEYOND THE SIMPLE REFUSAL”

“IN POLITICAL TERMS, REFUSAL IN ITSELF (OF WORK,
AUTHORITY, AND VOLUNTARY SERVITUDE) LEADS ONLY TO
A KIND OF SOCIAL SUICIDE. AS SPINOZA SAYS, IF WE
SIMPLY CUT THE TYRANNICAL HEAD OFF THE SOCIAL BODY,
WE WILL BE LEFT WITH THE DEFORMED CORPSE OF SOCIETY.
WHAT WE NEED IS TO CREATE A NEW SOCIAL BODY,
WHICH IS A PROJECT THAT GOES WELL BEYOND REFUSAL...
BEYOND THE SIMPLE REFUSAL, OR AS PART OF THAT
REFUSAL, WE NEED ALSO TO CONSTRUCT A NEW MODE OF
LIFE AND ABOVE ALL A NEW COMMUNITY” (HARDT &
NEGRI, 222)



--- Page 10 ---
“A PURE OUTSIDER"

“THE FORMULA, | PREFER NOT TO, IS NEITHER AN AFFIRMATION NOR
A NEGATION ... THE ATTORNEY WOULD BE RELIEVED IF BARTLEBY DID
NOT WANT TO, BUT BARTLEBY DOES NOT REFUSE, HE SIMPLY REJECTS
A NONPREFERRED (THE PROOFREADING, THE ERRANDS...)” (DELEUZE,
“BARTLEBY; OR, THE FORMULA” 70-71)

“IF BARTLEBY HAD REFUSED, HE COULD STILL BE SEEN AS A REBEL OR
INSURRECTIONARY, AND AS SUCH WOULD STILL HAVE A SOCIAL
ROLE. BUT THE FORMULA ... MAKES BARTLEBY A PURE OUTSIDER
[EXCLU] TO WHOM NO SOCIAL POSITION CAN BE ATTRIBUTED”
(DELEUZE, 73)

A “MOTIONLESS” MAN

“IN ANSWER TO MY ADVERTISEMENT, A MOTIONLESS YOUNG
MAN ONE MORNING, STOOD UPON MY OFFICE THRESHOLD,
THE DOOR BEING OPEN, FOR IT WAS SUMMER. | CAN SEE THAT
FIGURE NOW—PALLIDLY NEAT, PITIABLY RESPECTABLE,
INCURABLY FORLORN! IT WAS BARTLEBY.” (MELVILLE, 9)

“LIKE A VERY GHOST, AGREEABLY TO THE LAWS OF MAGICAL
INVOCATION, AT THE THIRD SUMMONS, HE APPEARED AT THE
ENTRANCE OF HIS HERMITAGE.” (MELVILLE, 15)

CONCLUSION

“| LIKE TO BE STATIONARY” (30)

i THE PASSIVITY OF BARTLEBY IS THE EXPRESSION, THE
PURE EXPRESSION OF HIS ONLY DESIRE: TO STAY WHERE HE
IS, ALWAYS.

BARTLEY DOES NOT WANT TO MOVE, OR TO BECOME: HE JUST
WANTS TO EXIST.

BARTLEBY TELLS US THAT A PART OF US DOES NOT WANT THIS
POINTLESS CHANGE, THIS UNIMPORTANT MOVE. BARTLEBY
REMINDS US OF THE STRONG TRUTH THAT SOMETIMES WE JUST
WANT TO EXIST WHERE WE ARE.
