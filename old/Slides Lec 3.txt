THE POLITICAL FUNCTION OF
NARRATIVES (3)

THE POLITICAL FUNCTION OF MORRISON’S WRITINGS IS
TO EXPRESS SOMETHING THAT WAS NEVER RECORDED OR
EXPRESSED.

MORRISON TRIES TO GIVE NAMES TO THINGS OR MORE
PRECISELY TO EVENTS WITH NO NAMES, THAT IS TO SAY
TRAUMATIC EVENTS.

IN THIS RESPECT, LANGUAGE IS THE ONLY MEDIATION
BETWEEN REPRESSED EVENTS, UNRECORDED EVENTS, AND
US.
