HORLA (2)

“A NEW BEING! WHY NOT? SUCH A BEING WAS BOUND TO

CONCLUSION

DELEUZE: A WRITER = SOMEONE WHO EXPERIENCED
SOMETHING BIGGER THAN HER.

MAUPASSANT HAD AN EXPERIENCE OF “THE INVISIBLE” :
HIS SENSITIVITY ENABLED HIM TO FEEL, TO SEE THINGS THAT
COMMON PEOPLE ARE NOT ABLE TO SEE.

FOR MAUPASSANT, LITERATURE WAS A WAY TO EMBODY
EXPERIENCES THAT ARE DIFFICULT TO DESCRIBE WITH
ORDINARY LANGUAGE (HENCE A WEIRD STYLE).
