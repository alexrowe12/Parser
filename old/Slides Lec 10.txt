The Nietzschean Overhuman is neither a superman, nor a
superhero: not a god, because the condition of the
possibility of the Overhuman is the death of gods; but not
human, in that he is not complaining about death and not
trying to escape this world, to escape death. The
Nietzschean Overhuman is an absolute earthly living being.

Zarathustra is not asking for weapons or technological dominion.
