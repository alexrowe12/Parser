FROM PRE-MODERN TO MODERN SCIENCE

“.. IF | COULD BANISH DISEASE FROM THE HUMAN FRAME, AND RENDER
MAN INVULNERABLE TO ANY BUT A VIOLENT DEATH!” (CHAP.2) = A
FANTASY (= AN IMPOSSIBLE DESIRE)

“THE ANCIENT TEACHERS OF THIS SCIENCE PROMISED IMPOSSIBILITIES, AND
PERFORMED NOTHING. THE MODERN MASTERS PROMISE VERY LITTLE; THEY
KNOW THAT METALS CANNOT BE TRANSMUTED, AND THAT THE ELIXIR OF LIFE
IS A CHIMERA. BUT THESE PHILOSOPHERS, WHOSE HANDS SEEM ONLY MADE
TO DABBLE IN DIRT, AND THEIR EYES TO PORE OVER THE MICROSCOPE OR
CRUCIBLE, HAVE INDEED PERFORMED MIRACLES. THEY PENETRATE INTO THE
RECESSES OF NATURE, AND SHOW HOW SHE WORKS IN HER HIDING PLACES,
THEY ASCEND INTO THE HEAVENS: THEY HAVE DISCOVERED HOW THE BLOOD
CIRCULATES, AND THE NATURE OF THE AIR WE BREATHE .” (CHAP.3) =
“MIRACLES OF MODERN SCIENCE

MODERN SCIENTISTS

RENE DESCARTES (1596 — 1650): THANKS TO MODERN SCIENCE,
HUMANS CAN BECOME “MASTERS AND POSSESSORS OF NATURE”
(DISCOURSE ON METHOD, 1637)

GALILEO (1564 — 1642): “THE BOOK OF NATURE IS WRITTEN IN
THE LANGUAGE OF MATHEMATICS” (THE ASSAYER, 1623)

Victor Frankenstein believes at the same time in modern
science and pre-modern science, in the microscope and in
magic. He believes that modern science will accomplish
the wishes of pre-modern science. He believes that modern
science will be able to bring dead people back fo life.

--- Page 16 ---
LATOUR’S ARGUMENTATION

1) “Our SIN IS NOT THAT WE CREATED TECHNOLOGIES BUT THAT WE
FAILED TO LOVE AND CARE FOR THEM. IT IS AS IF WE DECIDED THAT WE
WERE UNABLE TO FOLLOW THROUGH WITH THE EDUCATION OF OUR
CHILDREN.” (2);

2) THUS WE MUST “CARE FOR UNWANTED CONSEQUENCES.” (4);

3) “THE PROCESS OF HUMAN DEVELOPMENT [IS] A PROCESS OF
BECOMING EVER-MORE ATTACHED TO, AND INTIMATE WITH, A PANOPLY
OF NONHUMAN NATURES.” (2)

4) THE HATRED OF CONTEMPORARY TECHNOLOGICAL INNOVATIONS IS
EQUIVALENT TO A HATRED OF ATTACHMENTS BETWEEN HUMANS AND
NONHUMANS.
