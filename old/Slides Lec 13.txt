IN THE DEPTHS OF THE FOREST

“THE CALL [WAS] STILL SOUNDING IN THE DEPTHS OF THE FOREST. IT
FILLED HIM WITH A GREAT UNREST AND STRANGE DESIRES. IT CAUSED
HIM TO FEEL A VAGUE, SWEET GLADNESS, AND HE WAS AWARE OF
WILD YEARNINGS AND STIRRINGS FOR HE KNEW NOT WHAT.
SOMETIMES HE PURSUED THE CALL INTO THE FOREST, LOOKING FOR IT
AS THOUGH IT WERE A TANGIBLE THING.” (125)

“HE LOVED TO RUN IN THE DIM TWILIGHT OF THE SUMMER MIDNIGHTS,
LISTENING TO THE SUBDUED AND SLEEPY MURMURS OF THE FOREST,
READING SIGNS AND SOUNDS AS MAN MAY READ A BOOK.” (126)



--- Page 16 ---
ANSWERING THE CALL

“HE KNEW HE WAS AT LAST ANSWERING THE CALL, RUNNING BY THE SIDE OF HIS
WOOD BROTHER TOWARD THE PLACE FROM WHERE THE CALL SURELY CAME. OLD
MEMORIES WERE COMING UPON HIM FAST, AND HE WAS STIRRING TO THEM AS OF
OLD HE STIRRED TO THE REALITIES OF WHICH THEY WERE THE SHADOWS.” (128)

“HE IS NOT ALWAYS ALONE. WHEN THE LONG WINTER NIGHTS COME ON AND THE
WOLVES FOLLOW THEIR MEAT INTO THE LOWER VALLEYS, HE MAY BE SEEN
RUNNING AT THE HEAD OF THE PACK THROUGH THE PALE MOONLIGHT OR
GLIMMERING BOREALIS, LEAPING GIGANTIC ABOVE HIS FELLOWS, HIS GREAT
THROAT A-BELLOW AS HE SINGS A SONG OF THE YOUNGER WORLD, WHICH IS THE
SONG OF THE PACK.” (140)

To answer the call of the wild is not to come back to
the past or a will fo preserve the past: to answer the
call of the wild is to create a new future, it means to
sing the “song of the younger world.”

NATURALISM

¢ ART HAS TO BE REALISTIC, OBJECTIVE, QUASI-SCIENTIFIC.

e NATURALISM EMPHASIZES THAT THE ENVIRONMENT, BE IT
NATURAL OR SOCIAL, SHAPES THE HUMAN CHARACTER

IN THEIR NATURAL OR SOCIAL ENVIRONMENT, THE
