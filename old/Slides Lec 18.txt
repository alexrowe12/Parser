THE “SYMPATHETIC IMAGINATION”

Mrs. COSTELLO: “THERE ARE NO BOUNDS TO THE
SYMPATHETIC IMAGINATION.” (35)

1) AGAINST THOMAS NAGEL (A
CONTEMPORARY NORTH-AMERICAN PHILOSOPHER),
WHO ARGUES THAT WE CANNOT KNOW “WHAT IT IS
LIKE FOR A BAT TO BE A BAT’;

2) SUMPATHEIA: FROM XYN (SUN, “WITH,
TOGETHER”) + MAOOX (PATHOS, “SUFFERING”) = TO
SUFFER WITH. SYMPATHETIC IMAGINATION ENABLES
US TO INDIRECTLY FEEL WHAT NON-HUMANS LIVE.
